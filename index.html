Captain America: Civil War Tic-Tac-Toe

Whose side are you on?

Rules

  1. The object of Tic Tac Toe is to get three in a row.
  2. If there are two players, the youngest goes first.
  3. First player begins by selecting which square they want to claim and click on that square.
  4. Alternate turns claiming squares until either oppent has three in a row or all nine squares are filled.
  5. If a stalemate occurs, the board will reset and you can begin again.
  6. After a winner is determined, the board will clear and you can start again.
  7. In game 2, player 2 will go first, in game 3, player 1 will go first again, and so on.
